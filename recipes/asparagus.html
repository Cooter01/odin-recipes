Grilled Asparagus

Grilled asparagus is simple to make, but it lets you enjoy the natural flavor of your veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt an pepper to tase

steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the gate.
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to tase.
  3. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3 to 5 minutes.
